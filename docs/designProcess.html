Design Process

We followed a user-centric design process over the course of six weeks to identify an issue and iterate on a solution with our project partner, Brian Charlson. Through our conversations with Brian, we identified the accessibility of cooking resources as a problem that Brian and many others in the visually impaired community faces.

Design Documents

  • Project Proposal

    This document was written at the very beginning of our design process and contains our background research as well as our motivations for creating Sightless Chef. It also contains our initial design process plan.

  • Project Brief

    This document was written about halfway through our design process and contains a more specific challenge statement, our design and team goals, the intended impact of our product for two personas from different demographics, and a description and representation of our solution, Sightless Chef.
